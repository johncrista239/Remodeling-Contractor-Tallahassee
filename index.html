The Remodeling Process — What to Expect

Understanding the contractor’s process makes the renovation less intimidating. Typical steps include:

  • Consultation: Define goals, budget, and vision.
  • Design & Selections: Renderings, material choices, and schedule.
  • Fixed Proposal: A clear contract with milestones and pricing.
  • Construction: Demolition, trades coordination, and regular updates.
  • Inspections & Handover: Final walkthrough and warranty details.
